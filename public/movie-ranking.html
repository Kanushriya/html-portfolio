My top 3 favourite Movies

1. 3 Idiots

This movie is my all time favourite as it depicts life of an engineering student and the challenges he face in the racing life but didn't give up and studied practically to excel in life reality.

2. Uri:The surgical strike

This is a real life story of the incident happened in j&k where Indian jawans were killed on purpose by Pakistan. Later Indian government laid a mission to take revenge and got successful.

3. Super 30

This movie shows how Anand Kumar helped 30 poor but talented village students to achieve their dreams by qualifying engineering entrance exam.
